# Character Template

Use this template when creating new characters via `/character`. Not every field is required — start with what the user provides and leave the rest for later.

## Minimal Character (enough to start a scene)

```markdown
# [Name]
**Played by:** user | claude
**Created:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Appearance
[At least 2-3 distinguishing visual details]

## Personality
[3-5 core traits or a short paragraph]

## Voice & Speech
[How they talk — even one sentence helps, e.g., "formal and clipped" or "rambles when nervous"]
```

## Full Character

```markdown
# [Name]
**Played by:** user | claude
**Created:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Appearance
[Physical description, distinguishing features, typical clothing, body language habits]

## Personality
[Core traits, emotional patterns, how they act under pressure, contradictions, what they want]

## Voice & Speech
[Dialect, verbal tics, sentence length, favorite phrases, how speech changes with mood]

## Background
[Key history — formative events, relationships, what shaped them. Keep it functional, not encyclopedic.]

## Abilities / Skills
[Combat, magic, social manipulation, professional expertise, unusual knowledge. Skip if mundane setting.]

## Dynamics
[How they relate to other characters. Power dynamics, trust levels, tensions, soft spots, history together.]

## Notes
[Running character arcs, things to avoid, inside jokes, planned development, alternate versions.]
```

## Character Creation Questions

When walking a user through character creation, ask in this order (skip what they've already provided):

1. **Name and role**: "What's their name, and who are they in the story? (e.g., a detective, a rival, a love interest)"
2. **Played by**: "Will you play this character or should Claude play them?"
3. **Look**: "What do they look like? Even a few details help — hair, build, anything distinctive."
4. **Core personality**: "How would you describe them in 3-5 words? Or just tell me what they're like."
5. **How they talk**: "Any speech patterns, accent, verbal habits? How formal or casual?"
6. **Background** (optional): "Any backstory I should know? Just the important parts."
7. **Abilities** (optional): "Do they have any special skills, powers, or training?"
8. **Relationships** (optional): "How do they relate to other characters you've made?"

Present the finished character file for review before saving.
